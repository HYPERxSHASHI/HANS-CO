QUESTIONS

Pre-Order FAQ

Common questions about our pre-order process and product.

When will I receive my pre-ordered display? +

The first production batch is scheduled to ship in Q3 2025. You'll receive regular updates about your order status and estimated shipping date as production progresses.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, UPI (in India), and PayPal. Your payment method will be authorized but only charged when your unit is ready to ship.

Can I cancel or modify my pre-order? +

Yes, you can modify or cancel your pre-order at any time before your unit enters production. Once production begins, modifications may not be possible, but cancellations will receive a full refund up until shipping.

What's included in the warranty? +

All packages include our standard 1-year warranty covering manufacturing defects. Extended warranties are available with Creator and Collector packages. The warranty covers parts and labor for repair or replacement.

How does international shipping work? +

We ship worldwide with DHL Express. Shipping is free for all pre-orders. International orders may be subject to import duties and taxes based on your country's regulations, which are the responsibility of the recipient.
